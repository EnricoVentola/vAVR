Our story & sustainability promise

VAVR has spent decades engineering modular LED systems that can be refreshed, repaired, and reworked rather than replaced. We partner with integrators and brands that share our commitment to circularity and long-term value.

Rework. Repair. Refresh.

Our sustainability framework ensures that every project includes options for cabinet refurbishment, component recycling, and upgrade paths that reduce waste.

  • Fire-resistant resin technology with extended warranty
  • Component-level repair network in key global hubs
  • Lifecycle audits and refresh recommendations

Environmental credentials

We document embodied carbon, manufacturing practices, and packaging policies to support your sustainability reporting.

  • Closed-loop packaging with recyclable foam and cartons
  • QR-coded RMA process to streamline logistics
  • Energy-efficient processing and sleep modes

Innovation pipeline

From transparent all-in-ones to Videoflex® motorised systems, VAVR continually pushes the boundaries of immersive display technology.

  • MicroLED research lab with rapid prototyping
  • Transparent AIOs for experiential retail
  • Interactive floor engineering for rugged deployments
